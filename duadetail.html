Gratitude after Prayer

Category: Gratitude

Ya Allah, thank You for the strength to worship, for the peace in my heart after Salah. Even on the days when I feel distant or distracted, You still draw me near. The calm that follows prayer is a reminder that Your mercy envelops me, that You never tire of my return. I am grateful for the stillness You plant within me after every bow and every prostration. Let my heart always long for Your closeness, and may this act of worship never become a habit without presence. Ya Rabb, make me among those who find light and healing in every moment spent with You.
